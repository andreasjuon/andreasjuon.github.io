During my time as research associate at the Centre for Democracy Studies Aarau, I coordinated a team-based effort to update the Democracy Barometer, which comprises more than 100 indicators for 70 countries. My responsibilities also included data validation and management of the project webpage. The data can be found [here (external link)](https://democracybarometer.org/archive/).
