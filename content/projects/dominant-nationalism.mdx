# Dominant Nationalism Research Project

This project investigates the dynamics of dominant nationalism in contemporary political systems.

## Overview

Dominant nationalism represents a significant force in modern politics, shaping electoral outcomes, policy preferences, and institutional arrangements.

## Methodology

We employ quantitative methods including regression analysis, survey experiments, and computational text analysis to examine the mechanisms through which dominant nationalism operates.
