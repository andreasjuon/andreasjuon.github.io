# Power-Sharing Institutions

This project examines how power-sharing institutions affect political stability and conflict resolution.

## Research Question

How do different power-sharing arrangements influence the likelihood of conflict recurrence and democratic consolidation?

## Publications

This project has resulted in multiple publications examining various aspects of power-sharing.

## Data

The project utilizes original datasets on power-sharing institutions and conflict outcomes.
