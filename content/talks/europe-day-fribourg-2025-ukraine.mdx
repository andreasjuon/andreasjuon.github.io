I participated in a roundtable on Europe Day at the University of Fribourg on 9 May 2025, addressing the far-reaching implications of Russia's war against Ukraine for European security, political solidarity, and institutional order. Drawing on my research into conflict dynamics and minority rights, I contributed to the discussion of how the war has reshuffled European foreign policy alignments, tested multilateral cohesion, and raised fundamental questions about the territorial and political principles underpinning the post-Cold War European settlement. The event brought together scholars and practitioners to reflect on what "Europe after the turning point" means in practice for peace, security, and governance.
