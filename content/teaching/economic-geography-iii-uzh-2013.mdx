I taught this BA seminar in economic geography at the University of Zurich, Department of Geography (Geo242) in 2013–2014. The course examined the spatial dimensions of economic activity, regional development, and globalization. Students worked through theoretical frameworks alongside empirical cases to understand how economic processes are structured by and produce geographic patterns.
