I taught this BA seminar in economic geography at the University of Zurich, Department of Geography (Geo242) in 2013–2014, exploring the spatial dimensions of economic activity, regional development, and globalization. Students examined theoretical frameworks alongside empirical cases to understand how economic processes are structured by and produce geographic patterns.
