I taught this BA lecture at ETH Zurich (853-0010-00) from 2022 to 2024, examining contemporary armed conflicts, the challenges they pose, and potential solutions including peace negotiations, power-sharing arrangements, and post-conflict reconstruction. The course combined analytical frameworks with case studies of recent and ongoing conflicts to equip students with tools for policy-relevant analysis.
