I taught this lab course in statistics at the University of Zurich, Department of Geography (Geo246) in Spring 2014. The course covered descriptive and inferential statistics, data analysis, and quantitative methods tailored to geography students. Students applied these techniques to spatial and socioeconomic datasets using standard statistical software.
