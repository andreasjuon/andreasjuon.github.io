I taught this lab course in statistics at the University of Zurich, Department of Geography (Geo246) in Spring 2014, covering descriptive and inferential statistics, data analysis, and quantitative methods tailored to geography students. Students applied techniques to spatial and socioeconomic datasets using standard statistical software.
