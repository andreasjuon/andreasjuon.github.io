I taught this BA lecture at ETH Zurich (853-0015-00) from 2021 to 2024, introducing students to the study of political violence and covering major theoretical perspectives and empirical approaches to understanding the causes and dynamics of intrastate armed conflict. Topics ranged from classical theories of revolution and civil war to contemporary debates on ethnicity, state capacity, and foreign intervention.
