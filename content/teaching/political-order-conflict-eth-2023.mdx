I co-taught this MA seminar at ETH Zurich (857-0006-00L) with Prof. Lars-Erik Cederman in 2023–2024, examining the relationship between political order, state formation, and armed conflict. Students engaged with both classic and cutting-edge scholarship on how the organization of political authority shapes the onset, dynamics, and resolution of civil wars.
