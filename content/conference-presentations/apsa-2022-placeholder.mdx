At the 118th APSA Annual Meeting (Montréal, September 15–18, 2022), I presented this paper examining when and why dominant groups resist and retaliate against policies of minority accommodation. My paper was later published as "Ethnic Accommodation and the Backlash From Dominant Groups" in the Journal of Conflict Resolution.
