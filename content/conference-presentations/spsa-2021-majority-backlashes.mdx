At SPSA 2021 (online, February 4–5), I presented this paper analyzing when and why majority communities resist or react against policies designed to accommodate ethnic minorities. I examined the downstream consequences of such backlashes for political stability, arguing that they constitute a distinct and undertheorized pathway to renewed ethnic conflict.
