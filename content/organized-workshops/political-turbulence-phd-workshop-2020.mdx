I co-organized this two-day PhD workshop (February 24–25, 2020), bringing together doctoral researchers working on political instability, civil conflict, and state fragility. The workshop provided a forum for participants to present work in progress and receive structured feedback from peers and senior scholars in a collaborative setting.
